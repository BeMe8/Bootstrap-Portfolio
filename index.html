About Me

I am a front-end developer student based in London. I love creating web-wide wonders, designs you will fall for and cherish. Where imagination meets creativity and creativity meets code. I design dreams, one click at a time, and create enjoyable digital interactions and experiences that click and stick! I'm quietly confident, naturally curious, and perpetually working on improving my chops one design problem at a time.


I started my coding journey with the idea to make web designs that capture users and turn my imagination into virtual reality. I've always been drawn to both the creative and analytical side of development. I'm so thankful to be able to do both with the foundation I would build with Bootcamp’s education and support. I’ll be able to truly call myself a front-end developer who is currently transforming ideas into pixels with motive.
